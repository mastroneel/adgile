Our Mission

To solve one of advertising’s oldest and biggest challenges famously articulated by John Wanamaker:
“Half the money I spend on advertising is wasted: the trouble is, I don’t know which half.”

What we Do.

The Adgile technology literally watches TV. Through ADaptive Machine learning it understands, collects, and tracks every ADvertising message, and all program content on every Network in Real-Time. ADvanced high resolution image reception identifies ADverts whilst they are being broadcast. Brand, product, and offer identification takes less than 10ms.

Service Offering.

We are an end to end TV analytics suite. Our Real-Time data informs TV measurement metrics and delivers insights through customizable real time dashboards. Measure every conceivable TV performance metric against your own, competitor and total category TV activity.

Adgile also seamlessly integrates with all media buying platforms combining the strengths of TV and digital ADvertising through TV Reflex.

Our Data.

Unlike other attribution offerings, we need no data input from 3rd parties. Our proprietary technology delivers us the unique ADvantage of direct access to unparallel, accurate and granular TV data. We have no requirement to rely on other suppliers. We are an ADaptive data first offering.
